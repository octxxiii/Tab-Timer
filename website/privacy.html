Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Information We Collect

Tab Timer collects the following information:

  • Website URLs you visit (domain names only)
  • Time spent on each website
  • Basic usage statistics

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Tracking your browsing time
  • Generating usage statistics
  • Improving the extension's functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers.

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. Your browsing data remains private and stored only on your device.

Advertisements

Our extension and website may display advertisements through Google AdSense. These ads may use cookies for personalization, but we do not share your browsing data with advertisers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (through extension settings)
  • Opt-out of data collection (by uninstalling the extension)
