Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025
TL;DR
Receipt stores all data locally on your device. We collect nothing. We send nothing to any server. No account required.

1. Information We Collect

Receipt does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

The extension records the following data locally on your device only:

  • Domain names of websites you visit (e.g. "youtube.com")
  • Time spent on each website per day
  • Your streak count and usage goal preferences
  • Your Focus Mode block list configuration
  • Your language and theme preferences

This data never leaves your browser. It is stored using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API and is only accessible by the extension itself.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track page content, form inputs, passwords, or full URLs
  • We do not use cookies or third-party analytics
  • We do not have access to your browsing history beyond domain-level time tracking
  • We do not sell, share, or transfer any data to third parties

3. Permissions Used

Receipt requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • tabs — To detect when you switch tabs and measure time spent on each site
  • storage — To save your usage statistics locally on your device
  • notifications — To alert you when a time limit is reached or Focus Mode ends
  • idle — To pause tracking when you step away from your computer
  • alarms — To schedule Focus Mode auto-end and weekly report notifications
  • activeTab — To read the current tab's domain for time tracking

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data outside your device.

4. Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored locally via chrome.storage.local. You can delete all data at any time from the extension's Settings tab using the "데이터 초기화 / Clear Data" option.

Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data from your device.

5. Sharing Your Receipt

The "Share Receipt" feature generates an image of your daily usage statistics on your device. This image is copied to your clipboard. You choose where and whether to share it. We do not receive or store shared images.

6. Children's Privacy

Receipt is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
